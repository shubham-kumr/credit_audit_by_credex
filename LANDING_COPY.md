## FAQ

**Q: Is this really free?**
Yes. The audit runs entirely in your browser — no account, no credit card, no paywall. SpendLens is a free tool from Credex. If your savings are significant, we'll offer a free consultation — that's the only commercial step, and it's optional.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
All pricing is sourced from official vendor pages and verified with a date stamp (see PRICING_DATA.md). Vendor pricing changes — we update the data monthly. The audit shows official per-seat pricing so you can compare it against what you're actually paying.

**Q: Does my data get stored?**
Your spend numbers are processed in your browser before being sent to our servers. We store the audit results (with a random slug) so you can share the link. We never store personally identifiable information without your explicit consent.

**Q: What AI tools does the audit cover?**
Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Google Gemini, and Windsurf. These cover the majority of AI spend for engineering teams. More tools coming based on user requests.

**Q: What happens after the audit?**
You get a shareable results page with per-tool recommendations. If your team is saving more than $500/month, we'll offer a free Credex consultation — our team can often unlock additional savings through AI credits. There's no obligation.
