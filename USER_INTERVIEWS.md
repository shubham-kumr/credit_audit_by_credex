# User Interviews - SpendLens

Two informal discussions with friends and close professional connections conducted during development week. Each conversation lasted 10-15 minutes.

---

## Interview 1 - Rahul, Engineering Lead at B2B Startup (Series A, 35 people)

**Date:** May 22, 2026
**Connection:** University senior and LinkedIn connection.

**Background:** Rahul manages a 12-person development team. Over the last year, team members started expensing different AI tools on their own. The company has never run an audit on these tools.

**Direct Quotes:**

> "We are paying for Cursor, Copilot, and ChatGPT Team all at once. I honestly have no idea who is using what. When our finance lead asked me about it last month, I just said we were still testing them. In reality, we just haven't had the time to check."

> "I do not care about saving $50 a month as much as I care about having a quick, defensible answer when our finance lead asks for numbers. The real value is having a clean page to show them that justifies our spending."

**Key Insight:** The savings amount itself was less important to Rahul than having a shareable, credible report that he could send directly to his finance team to justify their decisions.

**Action Taken:** We prioritized the shareable slug URL as a major feature. The results page copy was changed from "Save results" to "Share with your finance lead" to match this job.

## Interview 2 - Pooja, Technical Founder at DevTools Startup (Bootstrapped, 4 people)

**Date:** May 23, 2026
**Connection:** College senior and active LinkedIn connection in the Bangalore tech network.

**Background:** Pooja handles both development and finances. In a small bootstrapped team, every dollar matters. They currently pay for Cursor and Claude Team plans but want to optimize their expenses.

**Direct Quotes:**

> "We literally share a Claude account to avoid the $125 per month minimum on the Team plan. I know it is not ideal, but per-seat pricing gets expensive quickly for small teams."

> "My problem is not that I do not know we are overpaying. It is that I do not have 30 minutes to sit down, open five different pricing pages, and calculate the cheapest option. I just need a tool that tells me exactly what to downgrade to."

**Key Insight:** Vague suggestions do not work. Pooja needed direct, actionable commands like "Switch from Claude Team to two separate Claude Pro accounts to save $85 per month."

**Action Taken:** We refined the audit outcome descriptions to recommend highly specific plan downgrades and seat changes rather than generic cost-cutting advice.
